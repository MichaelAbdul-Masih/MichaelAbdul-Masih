About me

My name is Michael Abdul-Masih and currently I am a postdoctoral research fellow at the European Southern Observatory (ESO) in Chile. My astronomy career began in Pennsylvania, USA at Villanova University, where I completed my undergraduate education (BSc) in May of 2014 with majors in Astronomy & Astrophysics and Biochemistry. I then completed my Masters degree (MSc) in Astrophysics in December of 2015 at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York. In the fall of 2016, I moved to Belgium where I completed my PhD in Astronomy at KU Leuven in May of 2020. Since then, I have worked at ESO as a postdoctoral fellow.
